16.2.2021 | 11.30am

i've fumbled with paper smiles so many times
the keys fall apart if we touch it.
so do i. so do you.
i tease the scarlet thread gingerly -
it unravels into strands.

they say that
fate and destiny
share the same sentiment,
that when the string strangles you
do not retaliate.

i scream a choked silence into an empty void,
and i tell you.
there is no reply.
