introduction

what's toki pona? honestly, if you don't know what toki pona is, i'm having a hard time trying to figure out how you found yourself here. but no matter.

what should i talk about in this time? maybe i should talk about one of the world's languages. maybe i'll toki pona. toki pona is toki pona, which means it's esperanto, which means it's spoken by ten people and has a mMm. the idea behind it is extreem vocabulary.

all in all, i think you should check out the conlang critic episode on toki pona by jan Misali - they'll be explain it way better than i ever could.
